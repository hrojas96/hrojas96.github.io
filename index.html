About Me
I was born and raised in Costa Rica. My first job was a teller at a state-owned bank while studying International Trade. At that time, my interest in computing was limited to what was necessary. Later, in search of improving my English and gaining new experiences, I applied for an exchange program that took me to Seattle, USA, where I lived for two years.

Immersed in such a tech-driven city and with extra free time during the pandemic, I began learning HTML and CSS on my own. It was then that I discovered a new passion in web design and technology, which led me to formally study Software Engineering.

Today, as a recent Software Engineering graduate, I am eager to expand my knowledge and gain more experience in the field.
